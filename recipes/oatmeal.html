Oatmeal

Home

Oatmeal is a delicious and simple staple which will warm your tummy and keep you full on those cold winter mornings.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quick oats
  • 2 cups water
  • 1/2 cup of your favorite fresh fruit cut into small pieces

Steps

  1. Pour the oats and water into instapot
  2. Cook on high pressure for 10 minutes
  3. Release the pressure
  4. Scoop desired serving into a bowl
  5. Top with fruit and enjoy!
