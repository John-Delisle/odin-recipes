Crustburger

Home

A thin burger,cheese and onion sandwiched in a flattened potatoe bun

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons softened butter
  • 1 potatoe hamburger bun
  • 2 ounces lean ground beef
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • onion slice
  • cheese slice
  • 1 tablespoon mayonaise
  • 2 teaspoons ketchup
  • 1/2 teaspoon hotsauce

Directions

  1. Butter the top and bottom of the bun then press them into a hot skillet until flat and toasted
  2. Roll the meat into a ball and then flatten it into the the hot skillet until cooked. Season with salt and pepper
  3. Place onion and cheese on top and wait for it to melt. Put together with the bun
  4. Stir mayonaise, ketchup and hot sauce together in a bowl and serve alongside the burger
