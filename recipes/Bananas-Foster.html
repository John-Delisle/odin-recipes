Bananas Foster

Home

Sliced bananas and walnuts in a buttery rum sauce over vanilla ice-cream

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 3 1/2 tablespoons rum
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 3 bananas
  • 1/4 cup chopped walnuts
  • 1 pint vanilla ice cream

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Stir in brown sugar, rum, vanilla and cinnamon
  3. Add bananas and walnuts. cook 1 to 2 minutes more
  4. Serve over vanilla ice cream and enjoy!
