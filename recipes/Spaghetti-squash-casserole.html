Spaghetti squash casserole

Home

Baked spaghetti squash, peppers and onions mixed with cheddar cheese and beef

  • 1 spaghetti squash, halved and seeded
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup diced green pepper
  • 1/4 cup diced red onion
  • 1 clove garlic, chopped
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 2 1/4 cups shradded sharp cheddar cheese

Directions

  1. step

    2. preheat oven to 375 degrees F

  2. step
    3. < p>Place squash on baking sheet and bake 40 minutes. Remove and shred pulp with fork

  3. step
    4. < p>Lightly grease a casserole dish

  4. step

    5. In a skillet over medium heat, brown beef. Mix in veggies and cook until tender

  5. step

    6. Fry squash, tomatoes and seasoning in skillet. remove from heat and stir in cheese. Transfer to casserole dish

    Bake 25 minutes in the oven. Sprinkle on some more cheese and continue baking until cheese melts
